À propos de moi

I’m originally from Cameroon, where I completed both my primary and secondary education — first at People Progressive Bilingual School, then at Collège De La Salle. Currently, I live in Italy, where I’m pursuing a degree in Electronic and Computer Engineering at the University of Ferrara. I’m now in my third year of study. I’m passionate about everything related to robotics and new technologies. I love challenges because they push me to test my limits — and go beyond them. Since March, I’ve been in Japan for an exchange program at the Shibaura Institute of Technology, which has been an exciting and enriching experience.
