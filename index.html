About Me

I am originally from Cameroon, where I completed both my primary and secondary education. I am fluent in three languages: French, English, and Italian.

Currently, I live in Italy, where I am pursuing a university degree in Electronic and Computer Engineering at the University of Ferrara. I am in my third year of study.

I am passionate about robotics and new technologies. I enjoy taking on challenges because they allow me to explore my limits and push beyond them.

Since March 2025, I have been in Japan as part of an academic exchange program at Shibaura Institute of Technology. This experience has been incredibly enriching, both intellectually and socially, offering me the opportunity to discover new perspectives and expand my international network.
